C E Y   H U M A N I T A R I A N  A W A R D

The Charles E. Young Humanitarian Award is an annual tribute to recognize and encourage projects that address the social needs of the community. To honor outstanding contributions and commitment to public service, and to encourage future projects that address social needs within the community, a $1000 donation to the recipients' community service project of choice is presented to 3 students at a reception in Spring Quarter.

You can apply at http://www.sole.ucla.edu/CEY-Award 

Email any questions or concerns to ceyaward@ucla.edu
