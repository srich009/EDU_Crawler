International Student Programs at UCLA

UCLA welcomes students from all over the world to join its educational community. Situated in one of the world’s most vibrant international cities, the university’s location coupled with its excellent faculty and extensive academic resources make UCLA one of the most desired educational destinations for visiting students. UCLA offers a wide variety of programs to help meet each student’s academic and professional goals.

  • Summer Study at UCLA
  • Reciprocity Exchange at UCLA
  • Undergraduate and Graduate Exchange through the UC Education Abroad Program (UCEAP)
  • Academic Year Visiting Scholars and Visiting Graduate Researchers
  • UCLA Extension

  • Graduate Admission to UCLA
