Parent News

Bruinlink Newsletter

Bruinlink, our digital parent & family newsletter, is published five times per year. This newsletter is designed to inform parents of growing university trends, college student development, and their role as a parent or guardian of a college student.

Bruin Family Insights

Bruin Family Insights is a  virtual discussion series designed to provide opportunities for Bruin parents and families to connect with and ask questions of UCLA staff, faculty and community members. View the latest discussion Understanding Your Students Summer Opportunities on YouTube Live.
