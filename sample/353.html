Chancellor's Associates
Donors who contribute $2,000 or more annually to any school, college, program or department are invited to become members of The Chancellor’s Associates.

These loyal supporters want to make a difference at UCR — and they do. Chancellor’s Associate members provide the backbone of critical private support that makes UCR a leader for positive developments in our community, our region and beyond. Their gifts make a profound difference in the lives of countless people locally and around the world who benefit from the UCR’s teaching, research and public service programs.

To learn more about the Chancellor's Associates level, please contact Deidre Stowell at (951) 827-4389 or donorrelations@ucr.edu.
