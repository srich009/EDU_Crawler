Message from Chancellor Block and EVC Waugh on immigration executive order

This past week, as most of you are well aware, President Trump signed an executive order that suspends entry into the United States for various categories of travelers. The order includes refugees, immigrants, non-immigrant visa holders, and possibly lawful U.S. permanent residents from seven majority-Muslim countries: Iran, Iraq, Libya, Somalia, Sudan, Syria and Yemen.

Get Involved

Membership

As a Bruin, you’ve earned your place as a member of our new UCLA Alumni Association membership program. Through us, your impact and connect to UCLA is amplified.  You have coveted access to benefits and opportunities to engage with your community.  Membership is your bridge to UCLA. Whether you live near Westwood or on the other side of the world, you’re encouraged to apply.

  • Learn More about Membership

Alumni Mentor Program

You are invited you to participate in the UCLA Alumni Mentor Program. This mutually beneficial relationship allows you make a meaningful impact on a current student while simultaneously enhancing your connection to the greater UCLA community. Whether you live near Westwood or on the other side of the world, you’re encouraged to apply.
