The Home (2017)

Friday, May 4, 2018
7:30 pm - 9:00 pm, UCLA Hammer Museum - Billy Wilder Theater

UCLA Celebration of Iranian Cinema

See below for additional information.

Admission

Advance tickets are available online for $10.

Tickets are also available at the Billy Wilder Theater box office starting one hour before showtime: $9, general admission; free to all UCLA students with valid ID; $8, other students, seniors and UCLA Alumni Association members with ID.

Additional Information

The Home (Iran, 2017) U.S. Premiere! Writer-director Asghar Yousefinejad knocks us off emotional balance right from the start of his debut feature with a grieving daughter’s explosive protests over the fate of her father’s corpse. Yousefinejad ratchets things up from there, confining the action to follow to the dead man’s home, where a parade of suddenly interested parties intervene, and what begins as a family drama, reveals itself as a sharp satire in which everyone’s motives become suspect. Winner of best film and best screenplay in the international competition at the Fajr Film Festival. DCP, color, in Persian with English subtitles, 78 min. Director/Screenwriter: Asghar Yousefinejad. Cast: Ramin Riazi, Mohadeseh Heyrat, Gholamreza Bagheri, Sedigheh Daryani.
