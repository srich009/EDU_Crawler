How to Park

Where to Park

Please refer to the appropriate campus parking map to find a parking structure convenient to your destination on campus, including accessible parking spaces.

  • Campus Parking Maps 
  • Patient Parking Information
 

Self-Service Parking Pay Stations

If you know where you are going on campus and need short-term parking, parking pay stations are fast and flexible.

  • How to use Self-Service Pay Stations 
  • Pay Station Rates and Locations

 

 

Daily Permits

Visitors may purchase all-day (daily) parking permits at an Information & Parking Booth on campus. Our attendants will issue you a parking permit and direct you to an appropriate parking lot or structure. They can also provide directions to campus attractions and answer any questions you may have. All-day parking may also be purchased at pay stations.

Please note: Information & Parking Booths only accept cash and BruinCard payments

  • Information & Parking Booth Hours and Locations 
 

Event Parking

Event Parking is often sold at the entrances of specific parking structures or areas near the event location. When this is the case, you will find clear signage directing you to the area you may park. Some events make special parking arrangements for their guests. It is always a good idea to check with the event sponsor for details. If no arrangements have been made, you may use one of the options listed above.

 

Refunds

No refunds are granted below the prevailing daily rate. Refunds are not provided for pay station parking.
