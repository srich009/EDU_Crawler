Cutting-Edge Scholarly Research

Through its research, the College of Humanities, Arts, and Social Sciences examines human existence, taking advantage of the vast scope of opportunities that exist in a large research university. Breakthroughs in the arts, humanities and social sciences happen on a regular basis. Exciting things are happening here, and we invite you to learn more.
