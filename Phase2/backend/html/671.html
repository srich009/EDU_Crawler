Results from placement and diagnostic tests are used to place students at the appropriate level in a particular course sequence. Students are required to meet placement requirements before they can enroll in mathematics, chemistry, English composition, and some foreign language courses. The placement examination is one method for meeting a placement requirement. Examination results are used for placement purposes only; they do not earn unit credit.

We require a placement test if you have any educational or familial background in the specified language. If you do not have a background in the language, you may fill out a survey confirming your language knowledge. If we can confirm that you do not have any significant knowledge in the language we will be able to permit you into the first course in the pattern. Come to room HMNSS 2416 to fill out the survey.
