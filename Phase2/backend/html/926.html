  2. Which UCR IEP housing option is right for me? [top]

    International Village (IV) (On-Campus Housing)

    International Village (IV) is our most flexible housing option for adult students (aged 18 and over) who want to live on-campus with or without a roommate/housemate. IV is an independently owned residence hall located on the UCR campus, within walking distance of your classes.

    Homestay (Off-Campus Housing)

    Homestay is the most economical housing option, and the best opportunity for cultural immersion. Students who stay with an American family improve their English more quickly, learn about American life and customs, and form a unique bond and friendship with their hosts. There is no minimum age and no minimum stay. You will have a double-occupancy, furnished room, and all meals and daily transportation to classes and school-related activities is included.

     

    Homestay (Off-Campus Housing) Fees
    Placement Fee $200 Nonrefundable
     
     
    Duration of Stay Rates
    Short-Term (1-9 weeks) $225 per person, per week; $33 per person, per extra day
    Long-Term (10+ weeks) $900 per person, per month; $30 per person, per extra day

     

    Notes:

    • The homestay placement fee is paid to the university.
    • The weekly/monthly room fees are paid directly to the families.

    Rental Apartments


    There are many options for off-campus apartments. Please remember to:

    • Never get an apartment through a "sublease."
    • Ask to see the exact apartment that you will be renting before signing a lease.
    • Only sign the lease when you understand all of the contract terms.

    A lease is a binding legal contract. It's important that you read through the contract (lease) thoroughly, and understand its terms, BEFORE you sign it.


    In California, your signature indicates your acceptance of all terms in a contract, and there is no time period to back out of the contract (called a "cooling-off" period), if you change your mind.

  4. I need a ride from the airport. Do you offer an airport transfer service? [top]

    Yes, we have an optional airport transfer service, if you fly into Ontario International Airport or Los Angeles International Airport. Prices and reservations are available here.


    Los Angeles International Airport (LAX) or Ontario International Airport (ONT) Arrivals and Departures


    Door-to-door service and check-in assistance is available through our optional Airport Transfer Service.


    UCR IEP Optional Airport Transfer Service Fees Los Angeles International Airport (LAX) Ontario International Airport (ONT)
    Reservation made 10 days in advance of arrival/departure. $100 $50
    Reservation made less than 10 days before arrival/departure $120 $70

    To apply for Airport Transfer at UCR IEP, you must first create an account and complete an IEP Program Application. After you have completed your program application, you can click here to request transfer from LAX or click here to request transfer from Ontario Airport. Only students admitted to an IEP program are eligible for applying for airport transfer.
