Hospitality Services

 

UC Riverside Hospitality Services provides the campus and surrounding communities with a variety of services and venues for social events such as weddings, anniversaries and birthday parties, as well as corporate, cultural and educational.

Our event coordinators provide planning, set-up, and technical support for numerous events per year. We have an excellent catering staff and extensive menus at reasonable prices to fit any occasion. Browse venues, plan an event, or make a reservation now with our staff room inquiry form or student room inquiry form.

Beautiful, Affordable Wedding Venues
The Alumni & Visitor’s Center and the Barn, our two wedding venues at UC Riverside, are ideal for intimate- and medium-sized weddings and receptions. Both venues are competitively priced with packages starting at $500.

Start Planning

Venues, Catering and Everything You Need
Business dinners, charity functions and holiday parties to name a few – Our event coordinators can create a package perfect for your special event at one of our campus venues. Services may include catering, audio/visual, guest parking, floral arrangements and specialty linen.

Let's Plan

Plan a Summer Conference at UC Riverside
Whether it’s a small or large group, UC Riverside offers competitively priced packages and comfortable accommodations for summer conferences and retreats. Summer conference packages include your choice of overnight accommodations, all-you-care-to-eat meals, and guest parking. For events, find plenty of additional meeting and recreational space for your entire group.

Stay the Summer!

Meetings and More!
Our venues feature a variety of meetings spaces that can be reserved for multiple- or single-part workshops, seminars, training sessions, presentations and other types of meetings. A wide-range of setups are available in each meeting space, creating flexible and comfortable spaces for participants.

View Meeting Spaces

Student Organization Events
Affordable meeting spaces are available to host your student events on campus. Based on availability, meeting spaces may be booked for single or recurring events. All meeting spaces include tables, chairs and most include audio/visual. We offer student discounts on meeting space reservations in certain locations.
