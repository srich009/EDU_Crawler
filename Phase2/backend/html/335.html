International Opportunities

Below are websites that provide information on a variety of abroad experiences, travel information, and programs.

http://www.state.gov/travel
Passport and visa info, tips for travelers, registration with embassies, documents needed, travel advisories, and country background notes

http://internationalcenter.umich.edu/swt/
University of Michigan's comprehensive list of resources to study, work, and travel abroad

http://www.goabroad.com/
Directories include study abroad, internships, volunteer opportunities, teach abroad, degrees abroad and language schools

http://www.transitionsabroad.com/
Work, study, travel and living abroad articles, advice, and resources for general information and specific locations/ programs

http://www.statravel.com
Discounted prices for youths and students for airfares, railway travel, international ID cards, international insurance, youth hostels, etc.

http://us.fulbrightonline.org/home.html
International exchange program offering opportunities for students, scholars, and professionals to undertake international graduate study, advanced research, university teaching, and teaching in elementary and secondary schools

http://www.miusa.org/ncde
Resource for people with disabilities, exchange and disability staff interested in study, work, intern, volunteer, research or teach abroad programs

http://www.barcelonasae.com/
Barcelona Study Abroad Experience (SAE) is the leading study abroad and internship provider in Barcelona.

www.bigbeyond.org
Opportunity to build skills and experience through international volunteer work placements in some of the world's most beautiful yet fragile places and the chance to connect with local people in need of their dedication.

http://www.globalexperiences.com/
Global internships experiences.

Teach Abroad 

http://www.worldteach.org
One year teaching programs. Must hold bachelor's degree

http://www.eslcafe.com
The internet’s meeting place for ESL and EFL teachers and students around the world

http://www.alliancesabroad.com
Customized internship, work, teach and volunteer programs for students and graduates

http://www.iss.edu
Assists qualified teachers and administrators in their search for opportunities in 200 American and international schools

http://www.goabroad.com/teach-abroad
Directory of teach abroad programs by country

http://www.reachtoteachrecruiting.com
Teach English in Korea, Taiwan or China

Work-Abroad Short-Term 

http://www.bunac.org/
Overseas work and travel programs

http://www.ccusa.com/
Overseas work and travel programs

http://www.interexchange.org/
Work, travel, volunteer, education, language school, au pair/childcare and internship programs

http://www.villagecamps.com/
International summer camps in 6 countries

Work-Abroad Long-Term 

http://www.jobsabroad.com/search.cfm
Search by country, type of job or most recent job postings

http://www.rileyguide.com/internat.html
The Riley Guide lists job announcements by country outside the U.S.

http://www.state.gov/m/dghr/hr/
Foreign Service - Information on the Foreign Service Officer careers and internships

http://www.state.gov/p/io/empl/
United Nations and Department of State list of employment opportunities and requirements. Most job announcements are for candidates with a graduate degree and 5-10 years of experience

Intern Abroad 

http://www.cdsintl.org/
Variety of internship, fellowship and study tour programs

www.eap.ucop.edu
University of California Education Abroad Program

www.studyabroad.com
Internships, work, volunteer, and au pair opportunities

http://www.globalroutes.org
Internship placements - college level teaching internships in Africa, Asia and Latin America

http://www.iaesteunitedstates.org/
International exchange, leadership training, global skill development, and technical education

http://www.goabroad.com/intern-abroad
Directory of internship programs by country

http://www.barcelonasae.com/
SAE Barcelona places students in internships across industries in Barcelona, Spain.
