                An Important Link to UCR's Past and Future

                Joining the UCR Alumni Association is a great way to keep in touch with classmates, find fellow alumni in related job fields or who live nearby, and stay up-to-date on the campus as a whole. For as little as $45 a year, you'll also receive a subscription to the Alumni Magazine, get discounts on entertainment, at UCR athletic events and at the UCR Campus Store and have access to all 10 UC Libraries.

                UCR provided you with the tools necessary to succeed in today and tomorrow's world, and we're continuing to grow. It's an exciting time to be a Highlander. Don't miss out!

                Wear Your Colors Proudly

                Show your Highlander pride by wearing official UC Riverside apparel from the UCR Campus Store. Our new Tartan Wear Collection features UCR's traditional Scottish plaid on shirts, skirts, ties, sweatshirts, hats, kilts and cummerbunds.

                Visit the Alumni & Visitors Center

                Established in 2007, the Alumni & Visitors Center is open to the public and features works of alumni authors in our library, an alumni art exhibit, meeting rooms and a formal boardroom. It is also home to the offices of Alumni & Constituent Relations and the Alumni Association. The facility is available to rent for special events, meetings and receptions.

                Make a Gift to UCR

                Gifts made in support of scholarships and graduate fellowships enable UCR to equip thousands of talented, deserving students with the skills they need to survive and thrive in the global marketplace. A scholarship can mean the difference for a young person trying to fulfill the dream of higher education. Through generous donations from people just like you, UCR has been able to attract the nation's brightest students, recruit world-class faculty, renovate classrooms and laboratories and provide support for UCR Intercollegiate Athletics and the Botanic Gardens.
