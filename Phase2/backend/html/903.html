Directions/Parking

The Financial Aid Office is part of the Highlander One Stop Shop (HOSS), located on the first floor of the Student Services Building, toward the center of campus and northwest of the Bell Tower.

Print a campus map!

We recommend you purchase an all-day parking permit for $6 at the Kiosk on West Campus Drive and park in Lot 6 as assigned by the attendant. Permits are required and parking regulations are in effect seven days a week on all UC Riverside property.
