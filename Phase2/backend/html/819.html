The William Henry Willis Memorial Poetry Prize

To Enter
The winning poem will be selected from all the poems submitted to Mosaic.  Therefore, all entries must comply with Mosaic submission guidelines.  No other application materials are necessary. 

Judging
During the spring quarter, the Mosaic editors forward all the entries to faculty judge(s), who make the final selections.
