Use our archives or contact us if you need access to records from more than a year ago. Hover over fee names to see descriptions and learn how each fee supports the dynamic students, programs and services that makes UCR a top-ranking university in affordability and value.

Keep in mind, these are estimates based on currently approved amounts. These figures may not be final. Actual tuition, fees and charges are subject to change by the Regents of the University of California and could be affected by state funding reductions. Final approved amounts (and a student's final balance due) may differ from the amounts shown.

The Student Health Insurance Plan (GSHIP) Premium is a mandatory premium for all students. The insurance supplements the outpatient care available to all registered students through the Campus Health Center. Students who can prove comparable or better coverage from another source may apply to be exempted from the mandatory plan and have the premium waived. This fee is non-refundable after the first day of instruction.

The Student Health Insurance Plan (USHIP) Premium is a mandatory premium for all students. The insurance supplements the outpatient care available to all registered students through the Campus Health Center. Students who can prove comparable or better coverage from another source may apply to be exempted from the mandatory plan and have the premium waived. This fee is non-refundable after the first day of instruction.
