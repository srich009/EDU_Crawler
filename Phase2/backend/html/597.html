First Sundays

Program: First Thursdays



First Thursday October - May, 6pm


On the first Thursday of each month, from 6pm - 9pm, ARTSblock is open to the public free of charge as part of the City of Riverside's Arts Walk. The Riverside Arts Walk is a free monthly community event that celebrates the diversity of arts and culture in Riverside and the Inland Empire. Visit over 22 downtown locations, including museums, galleries and studios, presenting an eclectic mix of visual and performing arts. Special events and performances are frequently hosted by ARTSblock as part of this First Thursday experience.




Program: First Sundays



First Sunday October - May, 1pm - 4pm


First Sundays provides high-quality, no-cost activities for families interested in having fun while enjoying historic Downtown Riverside's arts and cultural resources. UCR ARTSblock will be open every First Sunday of each month from October through May from 1pm - 4pm. Museum visitors will have an opportunity to view the current exhibitions. Our programs change periodically, so please check our exhibition schedule before you plan your visit.
