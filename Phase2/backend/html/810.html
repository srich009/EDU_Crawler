Christopher Buckley

Professor Emeritus

Christopher Buckley is a Guggenheim Fellow in poetry for 2007-2008. His most recent book is And the Sea from The Sheep Meadow press in New York. In 2008 his fifteenth book was published: Modern History: Prose Poems 1987-2007 from Tupelo Press. Alcatrez Editions publised Bear Flag Republic: Prose Poems and Poetics from California which he edited with Gary Young.
