UCR Farmers Market

Every Thursday (excluding holidays)
UC Riverside has it's very own weekly Farmers Market, held every Thursday from 3pm-7pm at the Flagpole lawn, just north of Hinderaker Hall. Come by and shop for fresh produce, baked goods, natural products, and more. A variety of vendors will be selling each week. For more info, contact us at UCRFarmersMarket@ucr.edu or follow us on Facebook and Instagram.



 
FarmShare at UCR
