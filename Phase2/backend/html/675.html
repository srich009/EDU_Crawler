How to Get Emergency Food at R’Pantry

You have options: 

 

  • Visit R’Pantry in person at Costo Hall 110, located on the ground floor of Costo Hall. Check facebook.com/ucrpantry for the most up-to-date walk-in hours (including grad-student-only hours).

  • Email us for an appointment at rpantry@ucr.edu.

  • Give us a call at  (951) 827-9355.

 

 

Frequently Asked Questions 

Who can get food?

Any student in need can access R'Pantry during walk-in hours or get individualized help by emailing us at rpantry@ucr.edu for an appointment.


What happens when I visit?

  1. First, you sign in at our check-in station with your ID.
  2. Next, you choose your items. You are allocated 10 "points" to use as currency during your visit. Most items are one point. You may use your points to choose up to eight food items and two personal care items. (Special items such as fresh produce and baby food do not require points.)
  3. Then, you’ll go to our checkout station where the R’Pantry staff will help you count your points and remind you of workshops and special programs (such as our box top collection program).


How often can I get food at R’Pantry?

You may visit R'Pantry once a week via any of our walk-in hours or by appointment. 

When is R’Pantry open?

Check facebook.com/ucrpantry for the most up-to-date walk-in hours (including grad-student-only hours).

 

What exactly is R’Pantry?

R’Pantry is the UC Riverside food pantry. It was created as a direct response to student need for more resources to fight food insecurity. Food insecurity is the limited or uncertain availability of nutritionally adequate and safe foods, or the ability to acquire such foods in a socially acceptable manner.

 

R’ Pantry aims to:
  • Provide emergency food to UC Riverside students in need
  • Connect students to on- and off-campus food resources
  • Improve the health and well-being of students through a variety of programs and workshops

R’Pantry is part of the campuswide food security program and is a partnership among Associated Students of UCR (ASUCR), the Division of Student Affairs, Feeding America and the UC Global Food Initiative.

How You Can Help R’Pantry

R'Pantry and its partners greatly appreciate all of the support
of our on- and off-campus community! Here are some ways that you can help:

 

Volunteer

  • If you are interested in volunteering as an individual or as a group, please contact the R'Pantry staff at a rpantry@ucr.edu

  • Common volunteer duties include unpacking large food boxes, stocking and organizing the pantry shelves, breaking down boxes and light cleaning. 

 

Organize a Food Drive
or Donate Items

  • If you are interested in organizing a food drive or donating items as an individual or group, please contact us at rpantry@ucr.edu.  

  • See the R'Pantry Wishlist for our most-needed and most-requested items. We will work with you to coordinate the details of your donation! 


 

Make a Financial Donation

  • If you are interested in making a financial donation, please visit our UCR My Gift page. 

  • If you are interested in an automatic payroll deduction, please fill out the R'Pantry Payroll Deduction Form and submit it to the UC Riverside Foundation in 1136 Hinderaker Hall.
