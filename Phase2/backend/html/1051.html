Application & Multimedia Development specializes in creating and integrating custom online applications. We also offer a robust assortment of graphics services related to application and interface design. But our multimedia services don't just stop there. Take a look at some examples of our work and you'll find a surprising range of distinct and custom web content, printed material and illustration services. In all respects, we take pride in supporting research, education, administration, marketing and public relations at UCR. Our services are available to faculty, staff, departments, organizations, and special interest groups affiliated with the university.
