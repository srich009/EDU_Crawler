The health of your mind, body, and spirit are integral to your personal and academic success.  The WELL recognizes that there are often barriers to maintaining your health and wellness, and we are here to help you overcome those challenges. 

We are dedicated to helping you navigate your way to better health!

 You've made it. You're here. You're at the WELL. Consider The WELL your personal one-stop oasis, a fountain of answers, support groups and mentors to help you refresh and reenergize body, mind and soul. Get help in a crisis. Find stress relievers. Begin a healthier lifestyle. Celebrate your individuality. Connect with other UCR students. You're at The WELL, now and we can't wait to help.
