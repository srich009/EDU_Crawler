Design Your Dream Wedding

 


Our wedding and reception packages are fabulous,flexible,and designed to fit your needs.  We are happy to host your wedding ceremony and reception, or reception only. Packages range from $32.00 - $65.00 per person. We look forward to meeting with you to discuss your very special day!

 

Wedding and Reception Packages

 

Wedding ceremony packages can be upgraded and customized to fit your needs.

A Standard Ceremony Package includes:
  • Alumni & Visitors Center, Patio and Lawn.
  • One-hour rehearsal ceremony on Thursday or Friday
  • One-hour garden veranda rental
  • Event manager
  • Bridal suite
  • Additional options available to customize your special day*
  • White resin chairs starting at $2.50/person*
  • White arches, gazebos or trellises starting at $65*


*Available at an additional cost.
Reception packages can be upgraded and customized to fit your needs.

A Standard Reception Package includes:
  • Alumni & Visitors Center Redmond Dining Room
  • Five-hour Redmond Dining Room rental
  • Banquet tables and chairs
  • Standard linen (includes ivory tablecloth and choice of napkin color)
  • All china, flatware and stemware
  • Wireless microphone and speakers
  • Engagement photo poster keepsake
  • DJ station


Additional options available to customize your special day:
  • Bar station and set up starting at $2.00/person* (alcoholic beverages are provided by the client – no corkage fee)
  • Dance Floor (9’x12’) starting at $130.00*

 

Wedding and Reception Venues

 
We have two venues perfect for intimate and medium-sized weddings and receptions. Venues can accommodate up to 150 guests.



The Alumni & Visitors Center
The Alumni & Visitors Center is a beautiful, modern venue.

The venue features the Redmond Dining Room, Arroyo Vista Patio & Lawn, as well as a bridal room and additional private rooms.

Group Accommodations      Take a 360° Tour


The Barn
The Barn is a casual, charming venue with a rich history. The venue, which was originally a real barn, was renovated and transformed and offers a unique architecture for your casual wedding and reception. The Barn features the Main Dining Room, West Dining Room, and Patio.
