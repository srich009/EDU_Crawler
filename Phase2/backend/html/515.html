Summer Sessions Fees Due June 15!

All Summer Sessions fees need to be paid by June 15. You will be dropped from your summer classes if you miss this deadline. If you enroll after June 15, fees are due the day you enroll.

summer.ucr.edu/fees.html


UCR has a session for everyone!

The following dates and deadlines are subject to change without notice. Please click on the colored buttons below to display important dates and deadlines for each session. 

Dates and deadlines per session:

Please keep in mind when enrolling in UCR summer courses:
♦You are responsible for all fees and grades associated with any courses that you enroll in.
♦It is your responsibility to monitor your enrollment closely, which includes dropping any unwanted courses/sections prior to the published drop deadlines! Do not assume that you will be dropped if you don't pay--if you no longer want to be enrolled in a class, you must go to R'Web and drop it!
