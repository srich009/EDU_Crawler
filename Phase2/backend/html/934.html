General Petitions for Graduate Students      

290 Petition (Petition for Directed Studies) 

Petition for one on one independent study (not for research); for research use 297/299 units
Add a Major

Current graduate students can request to add another major with the approval of the new program and their current program.

Change in Degree Objective

To change degree objective within same program

Change in Major

Current graduate students can request to change to another major with approval from the new program.

Education Abroad Program Academic Planning Form for Graduate Students

Advising form for graduate students who wish to pursue Education Abroad Programs.  Students must start by consulting with the UCR Study Abroad office.

Filing Fee Petition-Masters Students

For master’s students to apply for Filing Fee status

Filing Fee Petition-PhD Students

For PhD students to apply for Filing Fee status

General Graduate Student Petition

To transfer or waive units or course work; extend time limit for the removal of Incomplete grades, etc.

Grade Appeal (PDF or Word Doc)

To appeal a grade and given to the Academic Senate

Half-time Status and Reduced Fees App (for all except MBA students)

For those students who cannot attend more than half-time due to a full time job or for medical or personal reasons

Half-time Status and Reduced Fees App (for AGSM students only)

For students in programs within AGSM who cannot attend more than half-time due to a full time job or for medical or personal reasons

In-absentia Registration Application

For graduate students whose research or study requires them to remain outside California for the duration of a quarter

Intercampus Exchange Application

For those seeking to take classes at another University of California.

Late or Retroactive Enrollment Adjustment Form

For adding or withdrawing from units after the deadline

Leave of Absence (and/or Withdrawal) Form

For students to request from 1-3 quarters of leave from their studies

Withdrawal Form –use the Leave of Absence (and/or withdrawal) Form

For students who want to withdraw from the University

Petition for Remote Participation

To request that one member of the oral qual exam committee or the thesis/dissertation committee be permitted to participate in the exam remotely.
Readmission

Students who were enrolled in a graduate program for one quarter or more and have withdrawn from the University can apply to re-admit back into that same graduate program.

Report of Manuscript Completion

For Creative Writing and Writing for the Performing Arts students to report completion of their Manuscripts
S/NC Petition

For graduate students who wish to take a course for S/NC credit.
