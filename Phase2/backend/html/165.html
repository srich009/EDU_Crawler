Our kiosks sell parking permits for the current day only. If you need to purchase multiple day parking permits or parking permits in advance, please call or visit Parking Services.  Parking Services is located on campus at 683 Linden St.  Phone:  951-827-1294

Our kiosks accept cash, check, Visa, Mastercard and Discover.

AFTER HOURS PERMITS

For your convenience a Permit Dispenser is available and located at the University Kiosk.

  •  After hour permits cover evening and weekend parking.
  • This Permit Dispenser accepts Visa and Discover payment transactions.
  • Permits are valid in parking spaces and lots throughout the campus that are not otherwise posted as reserved, pay-by-space, disabled, or in any Housing parking lot.
