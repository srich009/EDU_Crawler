Plan Your Summer Conference at UC Riverside

 


Find overnight accommodations, dining and plenty of meeting and activity space all in one location at UCR. Summer conferences and programs are available from late June through early September.

 

Summer Group Packages

 
Standard Package
Youth and Housing accommodations are located at Aberdeen-Inverness and Lothian. Includes double and/or single occupancy rooms and communal, same-gender restrooms. Linen is available for an additional charge. 

Deluxe Package
Housing accommodations are located at Pentland Hills. Includes double and/or single occupancy rooms and suite bath and restrooms. Linen is available for an additional charge. 

Apartment Package
Great for professional conference guests, the Apartment Package offers additional privacy, space and amenities. Overnight accommodations are located at Glen Mor. Each apartment includes four single-occupancy bedrooms, two bathrooms, kitchen and living room. Linen is available for an additional charge.

Additional Ammenities

 

Meeting Spaces
A wide variety of meeting spaces are available for summer guests to utilize. Spaces range from large conference and auditorium style facilities to intimate breakout rooms. Most meeting spaces can be arranged in a variety of setups to suit your needs. Prices vary depending on room, audio/visual and setup. View meeting spaces.

Audio/Visual Equipment
Many of our meetings spaces are already equipped with basic audio/visual equipment. Additional fees may apply if room is not multimedia ready.

Dining
Two Residential Restaurants are available close to your  lodging accomodations for your dining pleasure. Each restaurant offers a variety of healthy, high-quality food options in an all-you-care-to-eat format. Additional snacks and meals can be purchased at other dining venues on campus.  Learn more about dining.

Catering
Morning fare,breaks,buffet luncheons, plated dinners — we offer catering services to conference guests for special events held during their stay. Learn more about having a catered event.  Learn more about having a catered event.

 

Our One-Stop-Shop Motto

 


We offer a three-tiered structured package for summer groups. Each package includes overnight accommodations, three meals a day, as well as access to exercise rooms and recreation fields.

Our specialties include:
  • Student/youth groups
  • Academic programs
  • Professional conferences
  • Religious groups
  • Sports and cheer camps
  • Campus departments
