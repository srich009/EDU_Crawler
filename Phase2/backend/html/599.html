                Campus Response Plan and services

                From an individual in crisis, to a dangerous situation that could impact campus safety, to an urgent condition such as an earthquake, fire or flood , UC Riverside is committed to a culture of compassion, assistance, coordination and prevention.

                In the event of an emergency, this Web site, as well as every other site in Student Affairs, will contain important crisis response information.

                The Emergency Warning Siren will be used to warn UC Riverside's community of emergency conditions on campus.

                Students, staff and faculty are also encouraged to sign up for UC Riverside's emergency notification system. This service enables rapid emergency communications to the entire campus community via a variety of technologies, including text messaging, e-mail, and voice mail.

                Information about emergencies at UC Riverside and related topics will also be available to the campus community through the following:
