Plan For Your Final Exams

Use this matrix as you make your class schedule to find out when your finals will take place. It's up to you to plan your class schedule so that your final exams won't conflict. Final exam times and locations are subject to change, so please consult the Final Exam Live Search ahead of finals week for the most up-to-date information.

  • If your regular class meeting time changes during the quarter, the time of your final will be based on the new class meeting time, not the original.
  • Although most finals are held in the same room where class is held, some finals may take place in an alternative classroom. Please go to Final Exams to use the dynamic calendar to confirm the location of your final. Check with your instructor for the location of your exam.
  • Types of final exams vary â€” your final might be a term paper, an in-class written or oral exam, a take-home test, or some other method of evaluation. Talk to your instructor about what to expect.
  • Read our Policy on Religious Accommodation if you are worried about finals conflicting with religious observances.
IMPORTANT!

Leading up to and during Finals Week, use Final Exam Live Search

The Final Exam Live Search provides the most up-to-date information on exam times and locations. On it, you can also sign up for alerts on any exam schedule or location changes.
