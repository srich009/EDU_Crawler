Job Listings in Different Industries! 

After you have looked at SCOTjobs here are additional job listing sites to consider. Please note that many of the links referenced are independent services. Their inclusion here does not constitute an endorsement, and the UCR Career Center is not responsible for their reliability, accuracy or content.


General Job Search Sites 

www.campstaff.com
Your one-stop destination for the best summer camp jobs at North America's finest camps.

www.careerbuilder.com
Searchable database of information from over 75 career sites.

www.careerjet.com

www.collegegrad.com
Entry level job listings for university graduates.

www.craigslist.com
Inland Empire and international online community where you can find listings for jobs and volunteering.

http://diversityjobs.com
A job search engine that finds job listings from company career pages, other job boards, newspapers and associations. With one search, we help you find the job with your name on it. Great scholarship options!

www.employmentguide.com

http://justjobs.com
A job search engine that finds job listings from company career pages, other job boards, newspapers and associations. With one search, we help you find the job with your name on it. Great scholarship options!

www.gettinghired.com
GettingHired is a national employment and social networking portal that uniquely connects job seekers with disabilities with employers committed to hiring them.

www.hound.com
Search job openings for jobs by industry and location. Job positions link to actual employer sites.

www.indeed.com
In one search, Indeed gives job seekers instant access to millions of jobs and internships from sources such as Monster, Hotjobs, Careerbuilder, Craigslist, the New York Times and Fortune 500 corporate Web sites.

www.latpro.com
Best jobs for Hispanic, Latino and Spanish and Portuguese bilinguals

www.monstertrak.com
Employers are here to hire college students and recent grads, thousands of Internship and Entry-Level jobs are available. Plus career advice and resources to help you succeed.

www.net-temps.com
Consortium of employment agencies; wide variety of entry level positions in California. (Note: We do not recommend utilizing employment agencies that charge the job seeker.)

www.mathjob.com
Can search job openings by location and industry; also links directly to employers' Web sites.

Job Search at TheLadders.com
We’re the largest online executive job board.  We offer a Career Advice section with many articles and tips for job seekers

SimplyHired.com, a leading job search engine, offers a personalized job search experience through its unique filters and social integrations with Facebook and LinkedIn.

CoolWorks.com
Offers jobs and summer opportunities in "great places" such as national parks and other outdoor types of positions.  

 

 

Art 

www.actorsequity.org/
The labor union representing over 40,000 American actors and stage managers working in the professional theatre. Provides job notices for actors and stage managers.

www.tcg.org/artsearch/
Searchable database of internship and employment opportunities in the performing and visual arts, including theaters and performing arts centers, summer festivals, educational institutions, dance or opera companies, symphony orchestras, museums, and more (login=artjobs@berkeley.edu, password=artsearch).

inlandempire.craiglslist.org/med/
San Francisco Bay Area online community where you can find numerous art related jobs listed.

www.planning.org/Jobsonline/
Includes companies, resources and job openings for creative services, artists and designers
