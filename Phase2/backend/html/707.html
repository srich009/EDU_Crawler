There's Always Someone Ready to Help You!

Sometimes UC Riverside can seem big and confusing. From finding the right classroom, to finding a place to live, to just finding a place to eat your life as a student has never felt more hectic! So in addition to the Campus Health Center, the Well, the Counseling Center, we have also assembled a list of our not-so well-known campus services and resources.

If you have a question or a problem be it academic, social, financial, health-related or whatever don't' hesitate to email or call. The office of the AVC/Dean of Students is always available to help!
