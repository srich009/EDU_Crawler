Mission Statement

To cultivate mental, physical and social development through recreational services, programs and facilities.

The Road to Fitness Starts Here

Our goal at the Student Recreation Center is to offer the widest variety of organized recreational activities to help you develop a positive attitude toward lifelong fitness —both mental and physical. Our knowledgeable staff is available at the front counter seven days a week to answer your questions.

Driving Directions

The Student Recreation Center is located on the north end of campus, off of W. Linden Street near Canyon Crest Drive. From Highway 60, exit University Avenue and turn left (east) onto University Avenue and turn left onto Canyon Crest Drive. Our building is behind the police station, next to the track field and Family Student Housing.

Parking Information:

SRC parking is available in any guest lot (i.e. 24, 30, and 22). Parking lot guest passes may also be purchased at the Parking Kiosk at the UCR entrance off of University Ave., and on Canyon Crest Drive off of Martin Luther King Blvd.
Please contact Parking Services for parking information.
