    Attention International Students

    Your visa status may prevent you from attending Highlander Orientation. Find out more

    Do I need to attend Highlander Orientation?

    I am an F-1/J-1 international student admitted to UCR and currently located outside of the United States. Do I need to attend Highlander Orientation?

    No, Highlander Orientation is not mandatory for F-1/J-1 international students currently located outside of the United States. According to United States immigration regulations you may not enter the United States until 30 days before the start date on your I-20 or DS-2019, so it will not be possible for you to attend Highlander Orientation. For more information, please contact Undergraduate Admissions no later than July 1 at 951-827-3411 or internationalinfo@ucr.edu.

    Your attendance is required at our Mandatory International Student Orientation event in September. Please be aware that International Student Orientation is a separate and mandatory event for all F-1 and J-1 visa holders. A detailed message about our Mandatory International Student Orientation Program will be sent to your UCR R'mail account in May. Learn more about Mandatory International Student Orientation. Contact International Affairs, International Students and Scholars at internationalstudents@ucr.edu or 951-827-4113.

    I am an international student currently in the United States. Do I need to attend Highlander Orientation?

    Yes, you are required to attend Highlander Orientation in addition to the Mandatory International Student Orientation in September. For more information, please contact Undergraduate Admissions no later than July 1 at 951-827-3411 or internationalinfo@ucr.edu.

    Your attendance is expected at both Orientation Programs. Please be aware that International Student Orientation is a separate and mandatory event for all F-1 and J-1 visa holders. A detailed message about our Mandatory International Student Orientation Program will be sent to your UCR R'mail account in May. Learn more about Mandatory International Student Orientation. Contact International Affairs, International Students and Scholars at internationalstudents@ucr.edu or 951-827-4113.
