Highlander Welcomes: Meet successful UCR alumni and recruiters while gaining career insight and networking with other recent graduates at one of the five events throughout California.

Class of 2018: Soon you will be part of the more than 110,000 Highlanders strong alumni network! Join the UCR Alumni Association to stay connected to your alma mater.

Have an award, new job, or milestone you want to share with us and your Highlander family? Let us know and we will consider it for the Class Acts section of an upcoming issue of UCR Magazine.

Looking to travel this year? Take advantage of the associations travel program with discounts on trips to Italy and France!

Legacy Brick Program: Celebrate a milestone or honor a family member! Proceeds from the Legacy Brick program support the Alumni Association Freshman Scholarship endowment, which provides $4,000 scholarships to incoming freshmen.
