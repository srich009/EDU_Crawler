Being Responsible, Staying Informed

UCR students are an integral part of our thriving campus community. How our students conduct themselves their behavior and actions in classrooms, residence halls, and social settings, as well as in the greater community has an effect that ripples throughout our university.

All students should read over and be certain that they understand the Standards of Conduct, which applies to all UC students, as well as the Academic Integrity policies and procedures. The office of the AVC/Dean of Students helps to enforce these codes and clarify their purpose for students, staff and faculty.

The office of the AVC/Dean of Students is also a resource for assistance in drafting petitions and appeals. Policies that impact students are provided below with direct links for your convenience.
