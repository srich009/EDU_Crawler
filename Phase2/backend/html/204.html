    Extending Your Stay

    • Your Orientation fee includes one overnight stay in an on-campus UCR residence hall. For example, if your session takes place July 9-10, your overnight stay in the Pentland Hills Residence Hall will be the night of July 9.
    • If you plan to arrive early for Orientation and would like additional overnight accommodations, Glen Mor Campus Apartments available. It is conveniently located next to the Pentland Hills Residence Hall, the location in which you will stay during your Highlander Orientation.
    • The Glen Mor overnight stay includes one twin bed and shared suite bathroom facilities. The cost is $55.75 per person/per night and subject to change. 
    • Please remember that if you choose to extend your stay and use the Glen Mor Campus Apartments, then you must move over to the Pentland Hills Residence Hall for your Orientation overnight stay.
    • Guest overnight accommodation fees are not included in the Family Orientation fees. Overnight accommodations are $55.75 per person/per night and subject to change. Registration and payment for the overnight stay must be sent to Housing (NOT the Highlander Orientation office). This is a separate process from the Highlander Orientation registration and payment process.
    • The Glen Mor option is also available to students who would like post-Orientation overnight accommodations. The cost is $55.75 per person/per night and subject to change.
