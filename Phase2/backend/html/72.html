Earn a World-Class Education


UC Riverside is a highly ranked research institution that is respected around the world. It is one of ten schools within the University of California system. Each year, international students from more than 65 countries come to UC Riverside.

RIVERSIDE,
CALIFORNIA

Riverside is located just 60 miles (97 kilometers) east of Los Angeles. It is the multicultural hub for arts and culture in inland Southern California. Riverside is ranked the “8th Coolest City” in the nation for entertainment, diversity, and recreation. It is also “California’s Coolest City” for its reduced carbon footprint efforts. (Forbes/Energy Services)
