Summer Sessions Fees Due June 15!

All Summer Sessions fees need to be paid by June 15. You will be dropped from your summer classes if you miss this deadline. If you enroll after June 15, fees are due the day you enroll.

Statement of Account

Access your statement and current account activity online, go to Student Accounts tab and click the "Student Account Online" link in R'Web.

The monthly Statement of Account includes all fees and applicable tuition, campus housing, other campus charges, credits for payments, waivers, and refunds or direct deposit transfers. Students receiving financial aid (must be enrolled) before financial aid funds can be electronically disbursed to offset charges on the statement. Payment for the amount due from the student plus any waiver or deferment must equal the amount due on the Statement of Account.

Late Fees Fees/tuition not paid in full by the "Payment Deadline" will result in a registration late fee of $50 and loss of classes. Housing payments received after the payment deadline will incur a $30 late fee or the amount specified in your lease agreement. Student Business Services will also assess a $12.50 late payment fee each month to accounts not paid by the deadline date indicated on the Statement of Account.  Deferred Payment Plan late fee is $50.00 for the1st installment and thereafter 12.50 per month until paid in full.

The University of California, Riverside (www.ucr.edu) is a doctoral research university, a living laboratory for groundbreaking exploration of issues critical to Inland Southern California, the state and communities around the world. Reflecting California's diverse culture, UCR’s enrollment is now nearly 23,000 students. The campus opened a medical school in 2013 and has reached the heart of the Coachella Valley by way of the UCR Palm Desert Center. The campus has an annual statewide economic impact of more than $1 billion.
