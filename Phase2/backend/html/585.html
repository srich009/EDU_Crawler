Why Consider Alliance Membership?

Alliance Partner companies join with the Career Center in providing professional assistance to UC Riverside students in the areas of career development, career employment and internship opportunities. Through their generosity, the Career Center is able to also provide special programming such as peer advising, virtual workshops, podcasts and other online resources.

Below are the four membership levels that the UCR Career Center provides as part of the alliance partner package. In addtion to our standard levels of participation, feel free to contact our office if you want a "customized" plan. We will work with your organization to design a partnership strategy that is unique.

 Partner Membership Levels 

  • Silver 
  • Gold 
  • Platinum 
  • Diamond 

Silver Partner - $2,000-$3,499

•One career fair registration of your choice at standard level.

•One direct e-mail advertisement to targeted student populations.

•Your company hyperlinked as employer of choice on Alliance member Webpage for one year.

•Recognition and acknowledgement as Alliance member in Career Center Calendar.

•Recognition as Alliance member on monitors in Career Center lobby.

•Special recognition designation on signs at Career Center events.

•Preferred participation in Career Center workshops and seminars.

•Advanced scheduling for on-campus interviews and company information sessions.

•Complimentary subscriptions to The Magazine of UC Riverside and Career Center Calendar (published 3 times a year).

Gold Partner - $3,499-$4,999

All benefits of Silver Partner plus:

•An additional career fair registration of your choice at standard level (2 in total).

•One direct e-mail advertisement per quarter to targeted student populations.

•SCOTjobs Web advertisement for one week.

•One creative event featuring your company such as an Open House, Coffee Chat, or Tech Talk.

Platinum Partner - $5,000-$9,999

All benefits of Gold Partner plus:

•An additional career fair registration of your choice at standard level (3 in total).

•Two direct e-mail advertisements per quarter to targeted student populations.

•One week as Featured Employer. Includes a customized consultation to make your Featured Employer of the Week events meet your recruiting goals. Branding examples:

  •  Prominent Banner on front page of Career Center website.
  •  Social Media Opportunity - Webinar, Facebook, Video Interview, Twitter.
  •  Creative event such as a Coffee Chat, Tech Talk or Open House.

Diamond Partner - $10,000 and more

All benefits of Platinum Partner plus:

•Sponsor-level registration at five career fairs throughout the year with prime location and when available, VIP parking.

•Name on Interview Room - Three spaces available.

•Spotlight in an additional issue of Career Center Calendar.

Thank you to the 2017-18 Alliance Partners
