Student Health

Covered by SHIP: As a student, you are automatically enrolled in UCR’s Student Health Insurance Program (SHIP) and exempt from purchasing a plan on the Covered CA website or your home state exchange. SHIP meets the requirements of the Affordable Care Act (ACA), gives you nationwide coverage, and offers the big advantage of convenient, on-campus care. Find out more about SHIP.

Campus Health Center

UCR’s Campus Health Center provides convenient, affordable and easily accessible medical care to students. All registered students are eligible to use the center. Students with the UC Student Health Insurance Plan have no out-of-pocket expenses for doctor’s visits. Students with private insurance will have a $20 fee for doctor’s visits. Lab work, X-rays, procedures and pharmaceuticals carry a range of fees. For appointments, call (951) 827-3031.

Services include:

    •   Emergency     •   Full-service Pharmacy
    •   After-Hours Care     •   On-site Laboratory
    •   Primary Care     •   Dental Clinic
    •   Walk-in Clinic     •   Health & Wellness Services
    •   Women’s Health     •   Health Education
    •   Travel Clinic

    •   Insurance
    •   Allergy Clinic
