Charles Whitney

Professor Emeritus
E-mail: chuck.whitney@ucr.edu

 

D. Charles (Chuck) Whitney is a former newspaper and wire service journalist, Whitney was, before joining the University of California faculty, on the journalism or mass communication faculties at the University of Texas, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, Ohio State University and Stanford University. The author or editor of six books and more than 80 book chapters, journal articles, technical papers and reports, Whitney is also an editor of the Encyclopedia of Journalism, to be published in 2009 by Sage Publications and is a current or former member of the editorial boards of 12 academic journals.
