Newspaper/Radio

Listen to native music and discussions. Get plugged in to American Indian news. Explore newspaper archives. Or join our NASP news team and write articles to develop your journalistic skills. Contact joshuag@ucr.edu to find out how to get involved.

Indian Time Radio Program

Tune in to the Indian Time radio program every Thursday from 5:50pm-6:30pm on KUCR, 88.3 FM - or stream live on kucr.org. Enjoy various genres of American Indian music, news, interviews, and discussions about past and present American Indian issues from students, staff, faculty, alumni, and community members. Every Thursday evening 5:30pm - 6:30pm.

Indian Times Newspaper Archive

Explore the Indian Times newspaper archives for articles about American Indian issues and news. The Indian Times is published by NASP, and can be revived with student help. Want to write articles or blog on native issues or news? Contact Josh Gonzales at joshuag@ucr.edu.
