Discover All That UCR Has to Offer You

Think of APSP as your first stop in discovering all the resources that UCR has to offer. Consider a major or minor in Asian American Studies. Find research materials you need at the Tomas Rivera Library. Get healthy in mind, body, and spirit at The WELL. Discover careers in your major at the Career Center. Whatever you need, we can help you find it.

Areas of Study

Make the study of your heritage part of your degree. Consider majoring in one of the following academic programs offered by UCR:

  • Asian American Studies
  • Asian Literatures and Cultures
  • Asian Studies
  • Ethnic Studies
  • Southeast Asian Studies

General UCR Resources

Find out about UCR’s cultural events, extensive libraries, botanic gardens, recreation options, concerts, and so much more at ucr.edu/resources.

Academic Resource Center

If you struggle with classes and grades, don’t get frustrated. Get help to succeed in your classes. acr.ucr.edu

Campus Advocate Resource & education (CARE)

Access resources, advocacy and educational programming related to issues of sexual assault, relationship violence and stalking.  care.ucr.edu  

Campus Health Center

Take care of your medical, vision, dental care and prescription needs right here on campus. campushealth.ucr.edu

Campus Map

Use a campus map to find your way around UCR’s 1,200 acre campus. campusmap.ucr.edu

Campus Safety Escort Service

Known as "the yellow shirts," these volunteers will escort you anywhere on campus, Sunday through Thursday, from dusk until midnight. escortservice.ucr.edu

Career Center

Find careers in your major, search for a job or internship, learn more about grad school, create a resume, and so much more. careers.ucr.edu

Counseling Center

You’re not alone at UCR. Get help with the struggles you face. Individual, couple and group therapy sessions are available. counseling.ucr.edu

Dean of Students

Serves as a student advocate while providing programs, services and resources for fun, health, and support. deanofstudents.ucr.edu

Dining

Discover all of your dining options. From retail restaurants to residential food courts and handy convenience stores, you’ll find something on campus to satisfy your hunger. dining.ucr.edu

Highlander One Stop Shop (HOSS)

Access UCR’s most popular services in one convenient location, on the ground floor of the Student Services Building.

  • Financial Aid
  • Registrar
  • Student Services/Main Cashiers
  • Undergraduate Admissions

Housing Services

Find out about housing options, move-in dates, resident handbooks and more. housing.ucr.edu

International Education Center (IEC)

If you are an international student, or want to study abroad, check out the world of services offered at the IEC. Internationalcenter.ucr.edu

Student Life

Get involved, join a student organization or attend a concert to see what Student Life has to offer. studentlife.ucr.edu

Student Recreation Center

Lift weights, pump up your cardio, take a fitness class, play racquetball, join an intramural team and more. recreation.ucr.edu

Student Special Services

If you have a disability, are a military veteran or dependent, or want to register to vote, this office has services for you. specialservices.ucr.edu

Transfer Resource Center

If you are a transfer student, stop by to study, use a computer, talk to a counselor, hang out, or relax. trc.ucr.edu

The WELL

Find resources and support on a variety of health topics, including nutrition, stress and sexuality. well.ucr.edu
