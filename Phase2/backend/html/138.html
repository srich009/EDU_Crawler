Team Challenge Course


Push Yourself. Challenge Your Team.

Maximize your organization’s confidence, communication and problem-solving skills with our “break out of the comfort zone” programs. All ages and levels welcome. Each fun-filled program includes:

  • Warm-ups – Games to overcome embarrassment and establish a safe learning environment
  • Initiatives – Activities to challenge the mind and enhance teamwork
  • Low and High Course – Activities to promote physical and mental challenge, creativity and team spirit

Questions?

Contact  ropes@ucr.edu.

 

  

R'Team (Low Course)

R'Team (Low Course)

Program length: 2.5 hours
Participants will attempt low-to-the-ground challenges using group effort. Personal reflection time and “team spirit” activities included.

 

Group Type Cost per person
UCR Student Organizations/Groups     $8pp
UCR Classes and Departments $12pp
Community $20pp

 

R'Challenge (Low & High Course)

R'Challenge (Low & High Course)

Program length: 4 or 6 hours
Effort – versus completion – is emphasized with our climbing wall, catwalks, giant swing and trapeze. Each gives a risky impression, but is as safe as any low-ground challenge. Achieve improved initiative and trust.


Group Type 4-hour program     6-hour program
UCR Student Organizations/Groups     $12pp $18pp
UCR Classes & Departments $18pp $25pp
Community $34pp $48pp

 

Challenge Course Climbing Wall 

Climb on up for fun, fitness and confidence building. (UCR student organizations and departments only.) Expert staff, safety gear, climbing shoes and an extreme sense of accomplishment included.

Group Type Cost per hour
On Campus Events/UCR Student Org.     $100

 

Try it for Yourself

Our Open House is just that – open to everyone! Climb a telephone pole. Jump for a trapeze. Take a giant swing, and learn how to reserve our Challenge Course for your next team building adventure. No experience necessary. All you need are closed-toe shoes and completed health/liability forms (available on site). Connect with us here or on Facebook to find out when we’re hosting our next Open House.

Find Us
Our Challenge Course is located behind the UCR Student Recreation Center (SRC) across from the A-I Residence Halls.
