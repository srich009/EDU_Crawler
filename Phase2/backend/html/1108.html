Schedule of Fees  

-- > Quarterly Schedule of Fees and Tuition for 2013-2014

Fees and tuition are paid by mail or in person at the Main Cashier's Office at the Highlander One Stop Shop (HOSS) on the first floor of the Student Services Building. For your statement of account, check Growl. Students will be notified via Web mail when their statement of account is available online. For payment deadlines, check the Calendar. Late registration fee (late payment) is $50.

See detailed descriptions of individual fees. Additional mandatory fees may be assessed. See Course Materials and Services Fee.
