A Al Qoyawayma Award For Excellence In Arts and Science To apply a student must:
  • 1. Be accepted or already attending either an undegraduate or graduate program. Consideration is also given to students enrolled in 2-year colleges.
  • 2. Demonstrate financial need
  • 3. Have proof of tribal enrollment
  • 4. Major in either science or engineering and have demonstrated an outstanding interest and skill in any of the visual, literary or performing arts.

Recent high school graduates, students already attending undergraduate or graduate programs, older adults returning to school after a long absence from formal educational studies are all able to apply.
