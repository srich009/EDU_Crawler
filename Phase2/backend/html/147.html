Mission and Objectives

The mission of the Planning and Budget Organization is to provide quality services and decision support to the campus community regarding the strategic use and allocation of campus resources, allowing UCR to achieve its goals and objectives. The Planning and Budget Division is committed to providing responsive, professional service to all constituents of our campus community.  We strive to provide quality support services and accurate and timely information so as to help the campus best manage all its resources.  We are committed to safeguarding and leveraging the university’s resources and assets.
