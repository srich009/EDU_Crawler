University Honors

University Honors is an enrichment program that engages a diverse community of students, faculty, and staff in the creation of global citizens through high-impact experiences that emphasize original scholarship, contribution, creativity, and innovation.

Visit University Honors Website >>

areas

  • Research and Creative Scholarship

    Students will conduct their own original research or creative project under the guidance of a faculty mentor which will culminate in a capstone project to be presented on upon completion of the program.

  • Faculty Engagement

    Opportunity to work closely with dedicated Honors Faculty Fellows from various disciplines. Our faculty dedicates additional time and resources to mentor University Honors students through their stay at UCR. In addition, with two Faculty-In-Residence, students can extend their learning capabilities beyond the classroom while living on campus.

  • Leadership and Professional Development

    University Honors provides a vast number of leadership and internship opportunities which include: Honors Ambassadors, Audeamus Editorial Board, Marketing and Media Team, and course Discussion Leaders. University Honors strives to enhance your preparation for post undergraduate careers.

    With over $300,000 in scholarships available annually, University Honors strives to support our students in their academic endeavors and professional development through these scholarship opportunities.

  • Community

    As a member of University Honors, students are part of a small and motivated community of scholars that supports each other through their journey at UCR.
