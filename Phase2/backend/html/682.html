Educate • Advocate • Empower

CARE (Campus Advocacy, Resources & Education) is an intervention and prevention support program dedicated to ending sexual violence at UC Riverside. We seek to inspire and cultivate a culture of care by providing resources, advocacy and educational programming related to issues of sexual assault, relationship violence and stalking.

 

 

 

 

Get Help

If you are a survivor of sexual violence, CARE advocates are available if you’d like confidential help in sorting through your emotions, resources and options. Learn about the services and resources our office can provide.

Education

CARE's mission includes educating the UCR community to promote a culture of consent and end sexual violence. On these pages we share resources, safety strategies and intervention techniques.

Programs

We recognize that healing can take many forms and can be different for everyone. These workshops and sessions are designed to help survivors discover their own path to healing.

 

Resources

If you're in need of assistance on campus or off, these resources can provide the help you need to feel safe and begin healing.

Get Involved

CARE offers these opportunities for students interested in working to create a safer campus for all Highlanders.
