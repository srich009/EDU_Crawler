Take Part, Get Involved!

Meet friends, develop skills and make a difference. Participate in our Lunar New Year Festival. Help plan a karaoke night. Create traditional origami cranes for a fundraiser. Help organize a social or workshop. Share your ideas and see them come to life. Work with other students and staff to build events and activities around the principles of leadership, consciousness and community. Become a leader through opportunities including:

Peer Mentor Program

Peer Mentors help first-year and transfer students make a smooth adjustment to college life and culture, direct them to UCR’s resources, and help them discover opportunities for personal, academic and social growth. Get paired up with a peer mentor or become a peer mentor to help other students.

Student Coordinator

Apply for employment at APSP as a paid student coordinator or volunteer to build your resume with work experience and skills. We also occasionally get internship leads and pass them on to our student workers.

Youth and Community Outreach Program (YCOP)

This APSP program offers UCR students the chance to mentor high school students in the Inland Empire. YCOP hosts monthly meetings where students discuss leadership development, race relations and other issues.

Leadership & Service Awards Nominations

The annual Asian and Pacific Islander Leadership & Service Awards Celebration recognizes the accomplishments of APSP students and affiliated student organizations. Nominate your peers and organizations to be recognized at the Leadership & Service Awards banquet.
