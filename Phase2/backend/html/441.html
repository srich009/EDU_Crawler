Welcome to the UCR Career Center

The mission of the UC Riverside Career Center is to connect, inspire, and empower UC Riverside students and alumni to become the most career - ready talent in the global workforce. 

Feel free to directly contact anyone on the staff for additional information on the services we offer students and employers.
