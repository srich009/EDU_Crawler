Ask Student Affairs: We're Here to Help

Are you having a problem with a roommate? Difficulty in school? Is there something about financial aid or registering for classes you don't understand? If there's any question you would like answered or something you want more information about, please don't hesitate to Ask Student Affairs.

Fill out the form below and a representative from Student Affairs will send you a personal reply. After you're done, click "submit." Be sure to include your e-mail address so we can send a response to your e-mail account!
