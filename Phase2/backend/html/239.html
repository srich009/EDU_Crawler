Mission Statement

  • In support of the University's goal for preeminence among U.S. research institutions, the Academic Personnel Office facilitates the recruitment, development, and retention of academic employees of the highest caliber.
  • We provide leadership, training, and assistance on policy issues associated with the employment and advancement of academic employees.
