Student Recreation Center Facilities

The Student Recreation Center (SRC) has a variety of conference rooms, gymnasiums and fields that are available for registered student organizations, campus departments and non-campus groups. With over 155,000 square-feet, you can reserve a state-of-the-art training room for 20 people or host a graduation ceremony for up to 3,000 people in the gymnasium. 

How to Make a Reservation

Click the Reserve button on this page and fill out the form.

For general questions regarding facility scheduling, please contact Rose Edmonds at Rose.Edmonds@ucr.edu or call (951) 827-4470. When you make an event reservation, you must complete an event process, which includes: event meetings, insurance verifications, equipment needs and risk management. Room rental rates are waived for registered student organizations, however, other fees may apply. Campus departments and off campus groups are responsible for facility rental and other applicable charges.

See photos of facilities at SRC North, SRC South and the Pool complex.

SRC Kitchen Reservation Policies


With expansion of the SRC, we now have more facilities available to rent. Listed below are the reservation policies for the SRC Kitchen Classroom for UCR Departments and Student Organizations.

  • All reservation requests must be submitted a minimum of 30 days in advance to Rose Edmonds, Manager-Recreation Activity Classes/Facility Scheduling.
  • Submitting a request does not mean a confirmation of the facility.
  • Reservations will be approved based on the availability of the facility, staff and type of activity.
  • The space can be reserved for meetings and cooking classes.
  • If the space is requested for a meeting only, the following rental fee structure will apply: (A.) UCR Registered Student Organizations – No rental fee but possible staffing fees may apply. (B.) UCR Departments – Rental fee $100.00 per hour plus possible staffing fees
  • If the space is requested for a cooking class/demonstration, the following rental and staffing fee structure will apply: (A.) UCR Registered Student Organizations – No rental fee but there will be a charge of $150.00 per hour for SRC Chef and use of equipment.  Only SRC payroll chefs may run cooking classes.  We must also have at least one staff member working that is Serve Safe certified. (B.) UCR Departments – room rental is $100.00 per hour plus an additional $150.00 per hour for the SRC Chef and use of equipment. Only SRC payroll chefs may run cooking classes. We must also have at least one staff member working that is Serve Safe certified.
  • Each group is responsible for all the equipment in the room. If there is any equipment missing or damaged, the group renting the facility will be responsible for all costs associated in replacing or repairing the equipment.
  • Each group will be responsible for keeping the room and equipment clean after use.
  • For cooking classes and demonstrations, there will be an additional cleaning fee of $250.00 so SRC staff can clean and sanitize all equipment as prescribed by EH&S and Industry standard codes.

Please, see the following notes and conditions for these facilities.

SRC North Pool Facility

  • SRC management reserves the right to determine the availability of pool areas for rent. Our #1 priority is open recreational use for SRC members.
  • 51% of participants must be UCR students to receive the student fee. If 50% or less, the rate is the same as department. SRC staff will determine the percentage of students.
  • Lap pool A or B are not available for reservation individually. Both areas must be scheduled together.
  • The spa is not available for reservation.
  • Request for usage of pool facilities must be appropriate recreational activities and consistent with SRC policies.
  • No glass allowed in or around pool facilities at any time
  • Rental times will include any set-up or tear down times needed for the event.

SRC North

  • SRC management reserves the right to determine the availability of courts for rent. Our #1 priority is open recreational use for SRC members.
  • 51% of participants must be UCR students to receive the student fee. If 50% or less, the rate is the same as department. SRC staff will determine the percentage of students.
  • The upper gymnasium is not available for reservation individually. It must be scheduled along with the main gymnasium.
  • The request for usage of the multi-purpose rooms and conference rooms must be appropriate recreational activities and consistent with SRC policies. No meetings, conferences or dinners.
  • Rental times will include any set-up or tear down times needed for the event. The charge is at the regular fee. (Minimum charge is 2 hours)
