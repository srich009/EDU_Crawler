With unlimited e-mail and file storage, server-side virus scanning of all e-mail messages, web-based access, and POP/IMAP access, UCR R'Mail e-mail accounts provide a high level of flexibility and security. As a UCR student, your student e-mail account is your primary means of receiving important academic information and is mandatory for all UCR students. As a UCR faculty member, it is easy to communicate and collaborate with your students. 

  • Professors and TA's can use R'Mail to notify students of class changes, exams, and other class-related information.
  • UCR academic and administrative departments will not use traditional mail (paper, stamps, and envelopes) as a primary delivery method. All student-specific information will be sent to student e-mail accounts.
