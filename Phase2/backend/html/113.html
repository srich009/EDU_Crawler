WELCOME TO UC RIVERSIDE HIGHLANDERS!

Download the UCRSRC Mobile APP Today! Visit go.ucr.edu/src and let Fit Happen for you!

Visit us at the SRC and let fit happen to you!

UCR’s newly expanded Student Recreation Center (SRC) is bigger and better than ever — and it’s all yours! As a registered UCR student, you are automatically a member of this on-campus, 155,000-square-foot facility.

  • 21,000-square-foot weight area: Includes 9 Olympic platforms
  • 100 cardio machines: With Internet and virtual courses
  • Fitness classes: Zumba, yoga, TRX and cycling offered daily
  • 237,000-gallon pool: Lap swimming, lessons and scuba
  • Drop-in: Basketball, volleyball and badminton
  • Intramural sports and clubs: Soccer, softball and flag-football
  • Outdoor Excursions Shop: Rent outdoor gear and reserve trips.
  • Recreation classes: Including martial arts, dance and bagpipes
  • Life Skills & Leadership: Receive the skills necessary to be successful.
  • "The Rock": 32-foot climbing wall and 12 ½- foot bouldering area.
