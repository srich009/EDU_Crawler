Our Role

Institutional Research supports UC Riverside with data analysis and reports designed to inform campus strategic planning and decision-making. We are the source of official statistics for the university related to students, faculty, and staff. Please visit our Campus Statistics page for the latest reports.

Have a request or question not found in our Campus Statistics tables? Please email us: ir@ucr.edu
