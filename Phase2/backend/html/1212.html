There's Always Something Going On at The WELL!

Bust the stress of midterms and finals with a nap session, or by petting a therapy animal at Therapy Fluffies. Be active and connect with others at PB&J (Peanut Butter & Jogging) every week, where we pair fitness with food and community. Check out our variety of Wellness Wednesday workshops each week.  Join your peers for sex talk in the WELL at our weekly Sex Ed & Chill discussion sessions.
These are just some of the exciting things The WELL has planned for you. We hope to see you there!

Don't miss out! Tips for staying connected:

  • Students, check your email for "Be Well" events in R'Side Weekly emails every week!  
  • Follow The WELL on Facebook to get program reminders and see photos from our events.

  • Request a PresentationHave somebody from
    theWell talk to your group.
