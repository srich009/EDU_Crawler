Data Requests

SATS provides data and reports on students, provided the request meets applicable federal, state, and university privacy laws and policies.

  • 1.To request data, complete and submit this online form
  • 2. SATS will send you and your supervisor an e-mail verifying SATS has received your request. We review all requests to ensure they comply with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA).
  • 3. SATS will notify you via e-mail with our decision. Approved requests take 7-10 days to process. If we deny your request, we will explain why via e-mail and suggest information to include if you resubmit your request.

Non-UCR Requests

If you're unaffiliated with UCR, SATS will charge a $100 fee to process your request. If you need assistance completing the data request form or have any questions regarding your request, e-mail the Data Request Coordinator.

Reports and Statistics Available on the Web

Institutional Research maintains a variety of standard reports and statistics for the public. Visit their website for access to enrollment planning reports, head counts, degrees conferred, student/faculty workload, student (academic) profiles and more.

Media Requests for Student Data

If you need student data that can be released to the public, direct your request to Institutional Research. Get a list of contacts.
