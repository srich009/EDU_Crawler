Find UCR Campus Housing

Our three housing options – Residence Halls, UCR Campus Apartments and Family Housing – provide you with a convenient, student-centered environment to complement university education.

With zero commute, friends next door and plenty of recreational activities happening down the hall or in the next building over, you are in the center of the action.

When it’s time to buckle down and study for a big exam, you'll find computer labs, study buddies and plenty of academic resources.

Plus, UCR Police and on-duty Residential Life staff provide 24-hour assistance and are committed to delivering a safe and secure environment.

When the campus is at your doorstep, the only thing you need to do is step outside.

Annual Security Report and Annual Fire Report

The Jeanne CLERY Disclosure of Campus Security Policy and Campus Crime Statistics Act of 1998, commonly referred to as the "CLERY Act," requires institutions of higher education receiving federal financial aid to report specified crime statistics on college campuses and to provide other safety and crime information to members of the campus community. These reports are available on the UC Riverside Police Department website and on the Fire Safety website.
