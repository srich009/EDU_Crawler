Student and Community Involement

Be part of the circle - get involved! Whether you have a little time or a lot, or prefer to be behind the scenes or in front, you have a place and a voice here. Participate in Native Jam Night. Help organize the Medicine Ways Conference. Set up tables for a beading workshop. Sell fry bread for fundraisers. Share your thoughts about native issues. It's a great way to meet people, develop leadership skills, and have fun!
