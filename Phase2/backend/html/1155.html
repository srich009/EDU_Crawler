UCR Resources

UCR has so much for you - we'll help you discover it all. Explore the native treasures in the Rupert Costo Library of the American Indian. Check out the Native American Studies undergraduate program. Get healthy in mind, body, and spirit at the Well. Take a fitness class at the Student Rec Center. Apply for Financial Aid - it's easier than you think. UCR has so much for you - we'll help you discover it all.



Of Special American Indian Interest

California Center for Native Nations

This UCR research center initiates, facilitates, and executes research by, about, and with American Indian people with a strong focus on California Native Nations. It also connects tribes with the expertise within the UC system to solve research problems unique to native nations. Find out more at http://ccnn.ucr.edu/ 

Native American Studies (Undergraduate)

Make the study of your heritage part of your degree. UCR offers Native American major and minor programs that examine the historical, social, economic, and cultural aspects of Native Americans. Find out more at  http://www.ethnicstudies.ucr.edu/undergraduate/area_of_study/NativeAmericanStudies.html

Rupert Costo Library of the American Indian

Visit the Special Collections section of the Rivera Library. Find out more at  https://library.ucr.edu/collections/rupert-costo-library-of-the-american-indian
