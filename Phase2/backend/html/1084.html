Explore Your Dining Options at UC Riverside

 

Whether you are hosting single day event or holding a multi-day summer conference, there are many dining options available on and close to campus.

 

Cater Your Event at UC Riverside

 

Citrus Grove Catering at UC Riverside is the exclusive caterer for UCR Housing, Dining, & Residential venues. They will handle all of your catering needs from coffee breaks and breakfast, to receptions, formal dinners and cocktail parties.

Our “One-Stop-Shop” will provide all your event needs including meeting space, catering, and any additional services and will be itemized on a master bill. Ask your event coordinator how to cater your event or preview the menu for some delicious ideas.



Fine Dining at Your Convenience
From beverage service and continental breakfast to fun-filled barbecues and gala receptions, Citrus Grove Catering is committed to providing the finest in menu selections and service to the university community. Choose from a full-range of culinary choices and catering services to fit almost any budget.

Citrus Grove Catering


On time. On budget. On Campus.
Add a tasty touch to your friendly or formal event with HUB2Go. Our fresh, housemade foods are perfect for office gatherings, parties, departmental meetings, club and organization meetings – you name it. Each platter serves 10-12. Visit HUB2Go for ordering information.

Summer Conference Guest Meals

 

Our summer conference room and board packages include three meals a day. Meals are served in our Residential Restaurant in Aberdeen-Inverness Residence Hall. This restaurant offers multiple food platforms in a casual, “all-you-care-to-eat” format. Additional dining options are available in our Market at Glen Mor and our Retail Restaurants, based on availability.

 

Dining on Campus

 

UC Riverside offers a variety of restaurants, markets and convenience stores, including Subway, Coffee Bean & Tea Leaf, Panda Express, Sushi by Panda, Starbucks and the Barn—a campus favorite. Each restaurant offers high quality, authentic and healthy cuisine. Visit our dining website to learn more.
