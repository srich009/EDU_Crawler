To get the most of your experience, it is best to start planning as early as possible.

Application deadlines are generally 6-9 months before departure dates. All degree-seeking, UC students are eligible to apply.

Steps to Study Abroad:

  1. Use our Study Abroad Builder to find the best program for your major, interests, career goals, and budget.
  2. Once you select a country and program, you can access specific application instructions.
  3. Register for a MyEAP account to fill out and submit the online portion of the application.
  4. Contact your campus Study Abroad office for questions about submitting your application and any accompanying documents.
  5. You should receive a status update by email within 8 weeks after the application deadline.
