37th Annual Medicine Ways Conference

Saturday May 5, 2018
9am to 8pm

INTN 1020/Arts Building Courtyard

"Think Native Buy Native: Indigenous Artivism and Entrepreneurship"

Admission is FREE and OPEN to the Public
FREE parking in Lot 1

The Native American Student Association, Rupert Costo Endowment Chair, California Center for Native Nations, and Native American Student Programs presents the Annual Medicine Ways Conference:

For over three decades, the Annual Medicine Ways Conference has been hosted by the Native American Student Association and Native American Student Programs at UCR. Featured guests from across the nation are invited to partake in this unique event. Various discussions and workshops emphasizing the respected theme are offered throughout the days events. Admission is FREE and food will be provided!

This year’s conference’s is organized about the theme “Think Native Buy Native: Indigenous Artivism and Entrepreneurship”. More details are to be announced as the year progresses!
