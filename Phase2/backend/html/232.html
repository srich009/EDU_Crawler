ARTSblock Membership


Experience all that ARTSblock has to offer as a member! Memberships offer unparalleled access to art and artists while providing essential support to the Inland Southern California’s vibrant cultural scene. Members enjoys privileges all year long, including unlimited free admission, private exhibition previews and daily viewing hours, discounted admission tickets for guests, valuable discounts and so much more.

All ARTSblock Members Enjoy


  • Priority Access
  • Free gallery admission for two
  • Discounted film tickets for two
  • Free admission to special evening events for two
  • Invitations for two to exhibition opening receptions
  • Reciprocal privileges at more than 30 university art museums
  • Free admission to lectures, seminars and discussion with artists
  • ARTSblock Tote Bag
  • 10% discount at the online store
