Aberdeen-Inverness

 


Located in UCR’s on-campus residential community, Aberdeen-Inverness Residence Hall features a variety of indoor and outdoor meetings spaces ideal for student groups and casual style meetings. Find plenty of space to host single or multi-room events. Overnight accommodations, and the signature outside Horseshoe, are available for use during the summer.
