MESC is your place for community!

Greetings and welcome to the MESC! From its humble beginnings as a student-founded ethnic and gender program, MESC has developed into a student center that offers a variety of resources including free printing, resume revisions, conference funding, internships, leadership opportunities, free coffee and tea, and much more! Visit us in HUB 377 to get involved and experience our vibrant, diverse and welcoming community! Follow us on social media to stay updated on all of our latest news, photos, events and projects!
