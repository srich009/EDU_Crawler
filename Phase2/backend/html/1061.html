Important Policy and Procedural Changes

Stay up on the latest policy and procedural changes that have occurred during UCR's transition to Banner. This quick reference is a work in progress, and will be updated periodically. Please note that it is not exhaustive. 

 


Banner Training

In-person, group and online trainings are taking place across campus. To find the right training for you, visit training. FERPA Training for Banner Users and Banner Navigation Training are required for all Banner users. To access these trainings, log in to ucrlearning.ucr.edu.  

 

 


Banner Feedback Form

Have questions or suggestions for improvement? Need to report a concern or give a compliment? Access our User Support pages or submit a Banner support ticket. Your information will be kept confidential, and we will make every effort to respond in a timely manner.
