Host Your Special Event at UC Riverside

 


Plan your special event, conference, workshop, meeting, seminar, training or symposium at UC Riverside by exploring our venues, event services and amenities.

 

Planning a Special Event

 
Versatile Meeting Spaces
Our venues offer a wide range sizes and types of meeting spaces for special events. Meeting spaces may be arranged in a variety of setups.

View Venues      Take a 360° Virtual Tour

 

Our event coordinators provide event scheduling and meeting planning services. Helping you each step of the way, our team will work with your budget, coordinate parking, as well as room set-up, AV/media and catering services.

Some of our venues feature breakout rooms that are attached or close to meeting rooms. Breakout rooms are useful for multi-part events or events that require preparatory space. An additional cost is applied for the use of any breakout rooms. Please inform your event coordinator if breakout rooms are needed.

UC Riverside Dining Services offers a variety of catering options, from a boxed lunch to a multi-course platted meal. Learn more about catering services.

Many of our meeting spaces are equipped with basic audio visual capabilities, included at no fee. Fees may be applied, if additional equipment is requested.

A majority of our meeting spaces come furnished with tables and chairs. Fees may be applied, if additional furniture is requested.
