The Benefits of Belonging

UC Riverside offers a comprehensive and competitive array of benefit choices. Employment benefits are an important part of your compensation package.

Most UC employees are eligible for benefits, but your benefits package depends on the length of your appointment, how many hours you work, and your appointment type. Retirees are eligible based on their age and years of service credit at retirement. Learn more about benefits eligibility.

If you are represented by a union, your compensation and benefits are governed by your unionâ€™s contract.

Be sure to visit our Benefit's pages to learn about the benefits the University provides, depending on your type of appointment.

What's New

UCR Receives the 2018 Mother-Baby Friendly Workplace Award

The California Breastfeeding Coalition recognizes employers for their support of lactating employees.

The criteria ensures compliance with existing California and Federal laws and includes: creating breastfeeding policies, ensuring employees are aware of their policies, and talking with expectant mothers about accommodating their breastfeeding needs when they return to work.

Nicole Vargas, Wellness Program Specialist, accepted the award on May 24, 2018, in a ceremony held in Sacramento. 

For additional information about the UCR Lactation Accommodation program, please visit the Lactation Accommodation program website. For more information about the award, please visit the California Breastfeeding Coalition website.

UC Employee and Retiree Eligibility for CALPERS Long Term Care Insurance

Following a plan promotion hiatus, Health and Welfare Benefits is now pleased to remind all UC employees, retirees and their relatives are eligible to apply for CALPERS Long Term Care insurance. We have published a basic overview of the benefit on UCnet > Other Benefits and includes links to the CALPERS website for more in-depth material.

On the CALPERS website, www.calperslongtermcare.com, UC employees and retirees will find comprehensive information including an introduction to the benefits of long-term care insurance, a cost of care calculator tool and choices of pre-packaged plans or customizable plans. Once an individual is ready to enroll, they may do so online.
ARAG legal Insurance: ARAG Legal App Now Available for Download

ARAG's new app, ARAG Legal, is now available to download from the iphone APP Store or Google Play and includes an attorney finder, digital ID cards and the ability to contact ARAG's Customer Care from your phone.

Have a legal issue? Use the Attorney Finder, with these helpful features, to locate attorneys near you who have experience in the area of law you need:

  • Ratings and reviews - see an attorney's overall satisfaction rating and detailed reviews from other plan members
  • Refine results - sort searches by highest rating, overall satisfaction rating, location, area of law, language, years in the ARAG network and more
  • Reduced feeds - even if your issue isn't covered, you still won't pay full price because network attorney's offer reduced rates; use the filter to view their discounted hour rate.

Watch this brief tutorial video to see the app in action.
