Marlan and Rosemary Bourns College of Engineering

Gain the innovative training needed to become a leading professional in your field.

Majors include:
  • Computer Science
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Bioengineering
  • Environmental Engineering

Learn More

College of Humanities, Arts, and Social Sciences

Be "at home in the world," exploring human meaning in social and historical contexts.

Majors include:
  • Economics
  • Psychology
  • Global Studies
  • Law and Society

Learn More

College of Natural and Agricultural Sciences

Improve the quality of life for those living in your community and around the world.

Majors include:
  • Biology
  • Neuroscience
  • Mathematics
  • Physics

Learn More

Graduate School of Education

Become a thought leader, addressing important issues with research-based solutions.

Programs:
  • Education minor
  • Teacher Education
  • Master's Degrees
  • Ph.D. Degrees

Learn More

School of Business

Learn how to address and solve the business challenges of the 21st century.

Seven concentrations include:
  • Accounting
  • Finance
  • Management
  • Marketing

Learn More

School of Medicine

Expand and diversify the physician workforce in Inland Southern California.

Programs:
  • Medical Education Program
  • Ph.D. Program in Biomedical Science
  • Graduate Medical Education

Learn More

School of Public Policy

Become one of tomorrow's leaders in effective, data-driven policy making.

Tracks include:
  • Health/Population Policy
  • Social/Cultural/Family Policy
  • Urban/Environmental Policy
  • International/Foreign Policy

Learn More
