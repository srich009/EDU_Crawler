Welcome -
Where Preparation Meets Opportunity!

We welcome you to the Early Academic Outreach Program (EAOP) at UC Riverside on the web. Please visit the who we are, mission, service schools sections to learn more the program. Do you need to update your contact information with EAOP?  If so, fill out this short form.

 

 

 

Interested in Joining EAOP?

If you attend one of our partner high schools or are a member of one of our community partners, you can start the EAOP application process by completing our enrollment survey. After submitting the survey, print and complete the required forms and waivers. Once our office receives the required waivers, we will contact you via e-mail to inform you about your eligibility for EAOP!

 

Summer Programs

College Prep Academy
July 9 - July 19

Students entering their sophomore or junior year are invited to take part in this two week academic enrichment program. Students will work with current UCR undergraduate students to challenge themselves in math, science, and English and will also get to learn more about majors and minors offered by colleges throughout California, career options for after college, and student life on a college campus.

Need to download an extra copy of the College Prep Academy forms? Click here!

Senior Summit
August 1 - August 3

Students in the class of 2019 are invited to attend our three-day, two-night residential program! Students will stay in the A&I residence halls at UCR and will work on their UC, CSU, and Private/Indepdent college applications. Students will also start the FAFSA application process, learn more about student life at college, and will even have the opportunity to participate in a talent show! Click the button below to start the registration process.
