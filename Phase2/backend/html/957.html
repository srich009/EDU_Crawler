Commuter Resources

 

Welcome Commuter Students!

More than 14,000 students commute to and from UC Riverside everyday, so if you’re commuting… you’re not alone. Student Life offers a variety of ways to help make commuting manageable, including social events, informational workshops on alternative transportation at Highlander Orientation, and a ton of online resources to access at your convenience. "Like" our Commuter Programs page on Facebook for up to date information on campus events.

Get Involved!

One of the best ways to get connected on campus is to get involved! Find information on student organizations on highlanderlink.ucr.edu or stop by an involvement fair in the Fall or Spring Quarters. If you are new student, get connected with a student mentor through the First-Year Mentor program. Contact Lizzie Brister at elizabeth.brister@ucr.edu to sign up for the mentor program.

Commuter Student Lounge

Meet other commuter students and take a break in between classes in the new Commuter Student Lounge. The lounge is located on the first floor of the HUB across from HUB dining locations and includes microwaves to warm up your own food and plenty of comfy chairs and charging stations!

New Student Commuter Welcome

One of our largest programs of the year is a welcome session for new commuter students. Held prior to Freshman Convocation, the Commuter Student Welcome is a great time to meet fellow commuter students, learn more about campus resources and programs and get connected with UCR Student Life staff!

Commuting Alternatives
Transportation and Parking Services (TAPS) offers carpools, van pools, campus shuttles and bike racks. Learn more.

Free Bus Rides
Check out bus routes that come from distances all across southern California — and all stop at UCR!

Off-Campus Housing
Provided by UCR Housing, the Community Living Program lets you access a database to off-campus housing options near campus.

Off-Campus Meal Options
Purchase "Bear Bucks" for your R'Card and swipe those dollars to eat in UCR's residential restaurants and all the other on-campus eating options.
