Like all residents, UCR students are expected to uphold all state and city laws and ordinances, including those related to noise, traffic, parking, zoning, and consumption of alcohol.

If you are having a residential problem with a UCR student(s) living off-campus, please fill out the form below. If you believe any laws may have been broken, also contact the Riverside Police Department at (951) 787-7911. If it is an emergency, dial 911.

For neighborhood parking, property upkeep, trash, and excessive renters, please contact Riverside Code Enforcement at (951) 826-5311.


 
 
 
 


 
Please include information such as number of individuals present at the house or apartment, number of cars parked on the street, specific disruptive or problematic behaviors, etc.
 
Please provide any information available to you that will help us in verifying whether or not the problem homeowners or tenants are UC Riverside students.
 
 
 
e.g. license plate numbers, UCR Parking Permit numbers
 
Complainant name, address, phone number, and e-mail address will not be shared with homeowner or tenants.
