Unit 18 Non-Senate Instructional Unit (IX) New Employee Orientation

The Non-Senate Faculty New Employee Orientation is designed to familiarize new employees in the IX unit with relevant information about working at the University and inform new instructors about the Union and what membership entails.

Moving Expenses Are Now Taxable

The new federal tax law treats all moving expense payments/reimbursement as nondeductible/taxable.
