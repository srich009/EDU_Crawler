Lothian Restaurants

 


Lothian Restaurants consists of two dining facilities within Lothian Residence Hall. Each serve fresh, high-quality meals and snacks throughout the day. Choose from Lothian Residential Restaurant for a variety of hot meals and healthy salads and tasty brick oven pizza, chicken wings, and other options.

 

 

Lothian Residential Restaurant

 


Featuring Seven Food Stations

Lothian Residential Restaurant offers continuous, all-you-care-to-eat dining in a relaxed, social environment. NEIGHBORHOOD GRILL
Serving rotating American diner classics, the Grill features grilled hamburgers, assorted sliders, melts, sandwiches, and all the sides and toppings plus French fries, onion rings and more.
