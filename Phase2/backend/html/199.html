Screening Room - Films
Evening films typically begin at 7pm, Saturday Matinee films begin at 3pm. Ticket prices for UCR ARTSblock's regular weekly film series are $9.99 per person in the evening, $8.00 for the Saturday Matinee, and $5 for students anytime.

DIRECTIONS TO THE ARTSblock

From 60-East (from Los Angeles)

[Mapquest may direct you to transition to 91-West and then exit Mission Inn Ave. However, it is easier to exit from 60-East, Market St. exit]

  • Take Market St. exit
  • Turn RIGHT off ramp onto Market St.
  • Drive past Mission Inn Ave.
  • Turn LEFT onto University Ave.
  • Drive past Main Street/Pedestrian Mall
  • Then take first RIGHT onto Orange St.
  • Turn right onto 9th St., drive short block, non-public parking structure on your left, turn RIGHT into alleyway, just before a yellow fire hydrant, then left into parking lot or loading dock area if making deliveries.

Parking lot rates are priced at reasonable rates and are in effect from 9-5, Mon-Fri. Parking is free before and after that time period and on weekends.
